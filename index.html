Dr. Srinivasa Ramanujan

The man who taught infinity

Here's a timeline of Dr. Srinivasa Ramanujan's life:

  • 1887 - Born in Erode, Mysore, India
  • 1920 - died at the age of 32. After his death, his brother Tirunarayanan compiled Ramanujan's remaining handwritten notes, consisting of formulae on singular moduli, hypergeometric series and continued fractions.
